A Foundation Model for Music Production

ROOM

Generate finished tracks, stems, and MIDI from a prompt. Export to any DAW. Send directly to REAPER with the connector.

Download App Try in Browser

Desktop App

The ROOM app connects to the hosted model, saves generations locally, and keeps your audio, stems, and MIDI organized.

Any DAW

Export WAV stems and MIDI files. Drag them into Ableton, FL Studio, Logic, Bitwig, Studio One, Cubase, Pro Tools, or REAPER.

REAPER Connector

Install the bundled ReaScript once, then click Send to REAPER inside the ROOM app to import generated audio tracks automatically.
